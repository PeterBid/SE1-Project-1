Instructions

- Move Vivi with the Arrow Keys to Start and Shuffle and Boogie to the Music!

- Dodge the Enemies and Reach All Four Air Ships to Win

- Whether you Win or Lose - Press Restart Game to Play Again with All Your Lives Back

- Watch out for the Time Bar as well!

Storyline

While Vivi and his fellow Mages were out exploring the Outer Continent, they were set upon by a group of Monsters and Magic-Wielding Bandits!

Although the Mages have a few spells up their sleeves, the Bandits numbers are too many to fight, and it's time to run...

Luckily Vivi's pet Bird Chocobo is on hand to help them make their escape.

Can you get Vivi and his fellow Mages to brave the Dangers of the Desert, escape the Bandits and Make it Back to the Air Ships In Time?

Hints and Tips

- You Must Ride the Bird to Reach the Air Ships!

- Line Yourself Up to a Gap while Riding the Bird and quickly Double Jump to Reach the Air Ship

- You Can Go All the Way Around while Riding the Bird. Use this to Jump at the Opportune Moment

- The Musicians are Slower than the Other Enemies use the Space Offered by them for a Quick Moments Respite

- The Sorceress and the Samurai are Fast! Quickly Jump your way Past Them
